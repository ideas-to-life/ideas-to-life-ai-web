## The Ideas-to-Life Path

This is the home for the experiments, the failures, and the breakthroughs that matter.

### 1. Intention First
We start with a human problem, not a feature list. Technology serves the intent, never the other way around.

### 2. Agentic Mastery
Where it is the right tool for the job, we orchestrate specialized AI agents to act as craftsmen, each an expert in their domain, working in concert.

### 3. Rapid Evolution
We ship prototypes early, listen to the loop, and refine with intent. Perfection is found in the iteration.

We build with the Product Triad in mind:
- **Feasible**: Can we build it with today's AI?
- **Desirable**: Do people actually want it?
- **Viable**: Can it sustain itself as a product?
